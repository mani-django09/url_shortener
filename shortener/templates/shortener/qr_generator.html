Dynamic QR codes allow you to change the destination content without creating a new QR code. Unlike static QR codes (which permanently encode the information), dynamic QR codes point to a short URL that redirects to your content. This means you can update where the QR code leads even after printing it. Dynamic codes also offer analytics to track scans.
